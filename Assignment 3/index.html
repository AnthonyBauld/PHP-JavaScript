1. What is Rock Paper Scissor's?

Rock, paper, scissors is a hand game in which each participant produces one of three shapes at the same time. It is normally played by two individuals. "Rock," "Paper," and "Scissors" are the three forms. A draw or a victory for one player and a defeat for the other are the only two possibilities.

2. How do I play Rock Paper Scissor's?

Choose one of the three symbols above, and the computer will choose a winner at random using rng. It will display a message and add +1 to either side depending on who wins.

3. How will I know if I won or lost?

A message will display when either you or the computer wins the game of Rock, Paper, Scissors!.
